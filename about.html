About Us

The Black, African-Heritage, and Caribbean Coalition (BLACC) is an organization dedicated to fostering unity, collaboration, and dialogue within the vibrant African-diaspora community here at George Mason University by hosting events such as "The Source", the "Black Excellence Gala", and more! We also pride ourselves on being a strong point of reference on what George Mason and/or BLACC has to offer affiliated organizations and its members.
