African Student Alliance Cultural

The African Student Association seeks to unite African and non-Africans, alike, by showcasing our diversity, correcting any misconceptions, and educating the Mason community of all the beauty in the continent of Africa.

Akoma Circle Cultural

Akoma Circle is a mentoring and service organization established through the Peer Empowerment Program in which Black freshman/transfer students are paired with upperclassmen. Mentors ease the transition to Mason for mentees while assisting with academic and social issues.

Alpha Kappa Alpha Greek

Alpha Kappa Alpha Sorority, Incorporated was founded on Wednesday January 15, 1908 on the campus of Howard University in Washington, DC. The Oh So Illustrious Omicron Iota Chapter of Alpha Kappa Alpha Sorority, Incorporated was chartered on the campus of George Mason University on December 5th, 1987.

Anointed Vessels of Unity Religion

We are an organization after God; our ministries consist of choir, praise team (a smaller singing group), praise dance, and miming. We minister at various churches and at on-campus events.

Black Student Alliance Cultural

We enhance the lives of Black Mason student academically, culturally, politically, and socially by instilling a sense of ownership and pride in George Mason University.

Caribbean Student Association Cultural

CSA is for students who share enthusiasm for the Caribbean due to Caribbean descent or an appreciation for the region. We are meant to be a haven for students to interact, learn, and express their love for Caribbean culture. We also raise awareness of issues and give back.

Chase Dreams Not Boys Support

Chase Dreams, Not Boys is a Women's Empowerment campaign geared towards fostering independent mindsets in young minority women as early as middle school. The mentoring and bonding of like-minded females will cultivate a supportive, loving, and driven environment which will naturally uplift and motivate all women involved to "chase dreams" and believe that they are attainable, no matter how grandiose.

Collegiate Black Man Support

The mission of Black Mason is to develop an open and accepting community of black men, who are academically accomplished, socially responsible, and career-ready.

Delta Sigma Theta Greek

Delta Sigma Theta Sorority, Incorporated is an organization of college educated women committed to the constructive development of its members and to public service with a primary focus on the Black community.

Egyptian Student Union Cultural

The Egyptian Student Union is an organization created to promote a unified community for Egyptians and those that are interested in the Egyptian culture on the George Mason University campus and its surrounding community.

Eritrean-Ethiopian Student Association Cultural

EESA is a cultural and geographical based organization that represents the countries of Eritrea and Ethiopia while creating a sense of community, and educating and enlightening others about our culture, history, and people.

National Association for the Advancement of Colored People Community Impact

NAACP GMU strives to create unity among the student population, eliminate race-based discrimination, and ensure the political, educational, social, and economic equality of minority group citizens.

National Association of Black Accountants Career

The National Association of Black Accountants (NABA) George Mason University Student Chapter seeks to address the professional needs of its members while building leaders that shape the future of the accounting and finance profession.

National Pan-Hellenic Council Greek

Our mission is the unanimity of thought and action as far as possible in the conduct of Greek letter collegiate fraternities and sororities, and to consider problems of mutual interest to its member organizations.

National Society of Black Engineers Career

Our mission is to increase the number of culturally responsible black engineers who excel academically, succeed professionally, and positively impact the community.

Phi Beta Sigma Greek

The brothers of Phi Beta Sigma are the Fraternity’s most valuable resource and strength. They are the primary means by which the Phi Beta Sigma objectives will be achieved. Sigma Gamma Rho Greek

Sigma Gamma Rho was founded on and continues to be steadfastly committed to the tenets of excellence in Scholarship, Sisterhood and Service. Sigma Gamma Rho has a proud legacy of providing positive and proactive community leadership and support when and wherever needed as indicated by the sorority's international slogan, "Greater Service, Greater Progress."
